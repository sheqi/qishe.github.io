Biography

I am a senior researcher at Tencent AI Lab. I finished my PhD study in Electronic Engineering Department of City University of Hong Kong, during which I have visited Center for Cognition, Vision, and Learning (CCVL) of UCLA.

I have been working on several projects across different subjects/areas by Statistical Machine Learning, including Computer Vision with Prof. Alan Yuille, and Complex Network with Prof. Guanrong Chen. My current/prior research interests include 3D Computer Vision, Neural Architecture Search, and Reinforcement Learning.

Interests

  • Computer Vision: 3D Reconstruction, Pose Estimation, Point Cloud Analysis
  • Machine Learning: Neural Architecture Search, Reinforcement Learning
  • Complex Network
